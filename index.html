Our Mission

Our mission is to create a reliable solar-powered charger that can track the sun and make renewable energy more accessible. We want to design a system that performs well outdoors, stays efficient in changing conditions, and helps people charge their devices wherever they are. Through this project, we hope to show how thoughtful engineering can make clean energy practical and easy to use.

Multidisciplinary Design

Our team consists of mechanical, civil, bio, and environmetal engineers as well as statistics and physics scholars. Each member contributes specialized skills such as CAD modeling, circuitry, and coding to help create our product that is both efficient and practical. We approach every stage of development with collaboration in mind, making sure every design decision supports both function and performance through our combined skill sets.

Our Values

As part of the Alpha Mu Engineering Team, we value user satisfaction and sustainlbel engineering our #1 priorities. Our team focuses on creative problem solving and accountability, encouraging members to take ownership of their actions and their impact on the world around them. Through this project, we show how leadership, teamwork, and innovation can turn engineering concepts into real solutions to real problems.
